Mobile App Design: Creptician

Creptician is a mobile-first platform that connects sneaker owners with skilled service providers for cleaning, customisation, and restoration. I designed the complete end-to-end user experience — from first interaction to final delivery — with a focus on making the process simple, transparent, and enjoyable. The UI balances a clean, modern aesthetic with elements of sneaker culture, ensuring it feels both trustworthy and culturally relevant.

The Creptician UI was designed to make booking sneaker cleaning, customisation, and restoration services feel effortless from start to finish. Users can identify what they need, compare providers, and make confident choices without friction.

Trust was a central focus, so the marketplace presents each service provider's expertise, reviews, and track record in a way that is both clear and visually engaging. The visual identity blends professionalism with the energy of sneaker culture, creating an experience that feels reliable yet culturally connected.
