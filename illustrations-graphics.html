Illustrations & Graphics

My approach to design brings together code, creativity, and visual storytelling. While I work with digital tools to build interfaces and experiences, a big part of my process also happens off-screen. I have a strong background in illustration and freehand drawing, which allows me to develop unique visual identities and add a more personal, organic layer to my design work.

Sketching by hand gives me space to explore concepts more freely and infuse character into everything from UI elements to branding. This combination of technical skill and artistic expression helps me create work that's both precise and deeply human.
